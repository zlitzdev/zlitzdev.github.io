About Me

Hello! I'm Vo Van Hieu, a Unity developer from Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam. Online, I'm also known as Zlitz. Over the past year, I've worked on several Unity projects, honing my skills and expanding my creative and technical expertise. It's been my dream to create games that people will love and enjoy.

I graduated from the University of Science in 2024 with a degree in Computer Science. Throughout my journey, I’ve been largely self-taught, diving deep into Unity development and exploring various facets of game creation.

My experience spans both programming and art. While programming is my strong suit, I also enjoy creating pixel art to bring visual charm to my projects. As a solo developer, I’ve worked on diverse areas, including game mechanics, art design, and even custom Unity tools to streamline workflows.

Currently, I’m seeking professional opportunities in the game development industry where I can contribute to exciting projects and grow alongside a talented team.
